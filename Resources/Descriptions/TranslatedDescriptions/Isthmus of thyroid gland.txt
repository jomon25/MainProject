

ISTHMUS OF THYROID GLAND


The isthmus is a thin band of tissue that connects the lower two thirds of the lobes of thryroid gland.

ISTMO DA GLÂNDULA TIREÓIDE

ISTHME DE LA GLANDE THYROÏDE
