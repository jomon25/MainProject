

INTERTARSAL JOINTS


The intertarsal joint are the joints of the tarsal bones in the foot. 

There are seven specific inter tarsal joints (articulations) in the human foot:

Subtalar joint
Talocalcaneonavicular joint
Calcaneocuboid joint
Cuneonavicular joint
Cuboideonavicular joint
Intercuneiform joints

ARTICULACIONES INTERTARSALES


La articulación intertarsal son las articulaciones de los huesos del tarso en el pie.

Hay siete articulaciones intertarsianas específicas (articulaciones) en el pie humano:

Articulación subastragalina
Articulación astrágalocaneoescafoidea
Articulación calcaneocuboidea
Articulación cuneonavicular
Articulación cuboideonavicular
Articulaciones intercuneiformes

JUNTAS INTERTARSAIS

Articulação subtalar
Articulação Talocalcaneonavicular
Articulação calcaneocuboide

Articulação cuboideonavicular

ARTICULATIONS INTERTARSALES


Les articulations intertarsiennes sont les articulations des os du tarse du pied.

Il existe sept articulations intertarsiennes spécifiques (articulations) dans le pied humain :

Articulation sous-talienne
Articulation talocalcanéonaviculaire
Articulation calcanéocuboïde
Articulation cunéonaviculaire
Articulation cuboïdéonaviculaire
Articulations intercunéiformes
