

RIGHT COLIC VEIN


The right colic vein drains the ascending colon, and is a tributary of the superior mesenteric vein. 

It travels with its corresponding artery, the right colic artery.

VENA CÓLICA DERECHA


La vena cólica derecha drena el colon ascendente y es tributaria de la vena mesentérica superior.

Viaja con su arteria correspondiente, la arteria cólica derecha.
