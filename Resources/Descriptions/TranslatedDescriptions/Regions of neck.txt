

REGIONS OF NECK


Beneath the regions of head, above the regions of thorax and the regions of back.

-Anterior region of neck
-Sternocleidomastoid region
-Lateral region of neck
-Posterior region of neck

-Región esternocleidomastoidea

-Región posterior del cuello
