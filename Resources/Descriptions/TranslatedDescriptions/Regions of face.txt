

REGIONS OF FACE


The facial region encompasses the lower half of the head beginning below the ears:

-Orbital region
-Infra-orbital region
-Buccal region
-Parotideomasseteric region
-Zygomatic region
-Nasal region
-Nasolabial sulcus
-Oral region
-Mentolabial sulcus
-Mental region
