

LYMPH NODES OF LOWER LIMB


All the lymph nodes from the inguinal region to the toes:
    
-Inguinal nodes
    -Superficial inguinal nodes
    -Deep inguinal nodes
-Popliteal nodes
-Superficial popliteal nodes
    -Deep popliteal nodes
-Anterior tibial node
-Posterior tibial node
-Fibular node
