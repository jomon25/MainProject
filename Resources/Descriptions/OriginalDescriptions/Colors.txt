

COLORS
    
    
Press 'Z' 
    to switch between 'Solid' and 'Rendered' viewport shading
    
In 'Solid viewport shading', 
Press 'Alt+Z' 
    to toggle X-ray mode.
Press 'Alt+B' 
    to create or get out of a Clipping border
    
Press 'Shift+Alt+Z'
    to set/reset the key colors of the muscles and their insertions.
    
Press 'N' to open the options
In 'Visibility', check 'Comic shader'
    to toggle the cartoon style
