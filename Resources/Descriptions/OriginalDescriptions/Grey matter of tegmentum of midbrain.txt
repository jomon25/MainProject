

GREY MATTER OF TEGMENTUM OF MIDBRAIN


It is a part of the tegmentum of midbrain, as the reticular formation of midbrain and the white matter of tegmentum of midbrain

The grey matter of tegmentum of midbrain includes:
    
-mesencephalic nucleus of trigeminal nerve
-nucleus of brachium of inferior colliculus
-parabigeminal nucleus
-nucleus of trochlear nerve
-oculomotor complex
-lateroposterior tegmental nucleus
-pedunculopontine tegmental nucleus
-nuclei of anterior tegmental area
-interpeduncular nucleus
-red nucleus
