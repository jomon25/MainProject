

LEFT PART OF LIVER


Located left to the main hepatic fissure (& the falciform ligament), the left part of liver is divided into two parts:

1-the left lateral division of liver

    The left posterior lateral segment of liver (II) and the left anterior lateral segment of liver (III).
    (II superior to the portal venous supply and III inferior).



2-and the left medial division of liver

    The left medial segment of liver (IV) lies lateral to the falciform ligament and is subdivided into IVa (superior) and IVb (inferior)
