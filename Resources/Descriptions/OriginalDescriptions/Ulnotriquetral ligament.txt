

ULNOTRIQUETRAL LIGAMENT


Together with the ulnocapitate ligament, the ulnolunate ligament and the ulnopisiform ligament, they form the palmar ulnocarpal ligament.

The palmar ulnocarpal ligament is one of the major ligaments of the wrist joint, as the:
    
-dorsal radiocarpal ligament
-palmar radiocarpal ligament
-dorsal ulnocarpal ligament
-palmar ulnocarpal ligament

and the
-ulnar collateral ligament of wrist joint
-radial collateral ligament of wrist joint
