

COCCYGEAL NERVE


The bilateral coccygeal nerves, Co, are the 31st pair of spinal nerves. 

It arises from the conus medullaris, and its ventral ramus helps form the coccygeal plexus. 

It does not divide into a medial and lateral branch. 

Its fibers are distributed to the skin superficial and posterior to the coccyx bone via the anococcygeal nerve of the coccygeal nerve plexus.
