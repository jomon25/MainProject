

ULNOPISIFORM LIGAMENT


The ulnopisiform ligament if a part of the palmar ulnocarpal ligament, as the:

-Ulnocapitate ligament
-Ulnolunate ligament
-Ulnotriquetral ligament

The palmar ulnocarpal ligament is one of the major ligaments of the radiocarpal joint (wrist) as the:

-Dorsal radiocarpal ligament
-Palmar radiocarpal ligament
-Dorsal ulnocarpal ligament

and the
-Ulnar collateral ligament of wrist joint
-Radial collateral ligament of wrist joint
