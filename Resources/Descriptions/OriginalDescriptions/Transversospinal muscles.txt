TRANSVERSOSPINALES


The transversospinales are a group of muscles of the human back. 

Their combined action is rotation and extension of the vertebral column. 

These muscles are small and have a poor mechanical advantage for contributing to motion. 

They include:

-the three semispinalis muscles, spanning 4-6 vertebral segments
    -semispinalis thoracis
    -semispinalis cervicis
    -semispinalis capitis

-multifidus, spanning 2-4 vertebral segments

-rotatores, spanning 1-2 vertebral segments
    -rotatores cervicis
    -rotatores thoracis
    -rotatores lumborum
