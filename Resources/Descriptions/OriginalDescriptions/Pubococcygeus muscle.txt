

PUBOCOCCYGEUS MUSCLE


The levator ani is made up of 3 parts:

    Iliococcygeus muscle
    Pubococcygeus muscle
    Puborectalis muscle

The pubococcygeus muscle has medial fibres forming the pubovaginalis in the female, and the puboprostaticus in the male.
