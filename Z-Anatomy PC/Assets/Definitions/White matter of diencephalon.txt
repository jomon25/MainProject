

WHITE MATTER OF DIENCEPHALON


The white matter of the diencephalon includes:
    
-stria medullaris thalami
-habenular commissure
-posterior commissure
-habenulo-interpeduncular tract
-ansa lenticularis
-lenticular fasciculus
-ansa peduncularis
-fasciculus peduncularis
-fasciculus subthalamicus
-fasciculus thalamicus
-mamillothalamic fasciculus
-mamillotegmental fasciculus
-supra-optic commissures
-periventricular fibres of thalamus
-periventricular fibres of hypothalamus
-medial forebrain bundle
-hypothalamohypophysial tract
-retinohypothalamic tract
