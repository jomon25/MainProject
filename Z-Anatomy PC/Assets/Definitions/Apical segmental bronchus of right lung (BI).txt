

APICAL SEGMENTAL BRONCHUS OF RIGHT LUNG (BI)


For the apical segment of right lung, that extends until the third rib bellow.

BRONCO SEGMENTAL APICAL DO PULMÃO DIREITO (BI)

BRONCHE SEGMENTAIRE APICALE DU POUMON DROIT (BI)
