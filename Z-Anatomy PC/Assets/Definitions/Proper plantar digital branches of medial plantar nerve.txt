

PROPER PLANTAR DIGITAL BRANCHES OF MEDIAL PLANTAR NERVE


Plantar cutaneous nerves for the fibular and tibial sides of the 3,5 medial toes.

They also innervate the the distal phalanges on the dorsal side.
