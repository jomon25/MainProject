

OVARIAN PLEXUS


The ovarian plexus arises from the renal plexus, and is distributed to the ovary, and fundus of the uterus.
It is carried in the suspensory ligament of the ovary.

PLEXO OVÁRICO


El plexo ovárico surge del plexo renal y se distribuye al ovario y al fondo del útero.
Se lleva en el ligamento suspensorio del ovario.
